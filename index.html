About Our Company

Maritime Technical & Support Services (Pvt) Ltd (MTSS) was established in September 2010 through Security and Exchange Commission of Pakistan. Registered with DGDP, Directorate of Procurement (Navy), Directorate of Procurement (Air), HQ COMLOG & PMSA. The Company's primary role is to assist Pakistan Navy and Maritime organizations in technical and logistic support for Ships, Submarines and Aircraft in addition to support other organizations.

Maritime Technical & Support Services Business

MTSS(Pvt) Ltd is primarily providing support to Pakistan Navy in the areas of project management, contract execution, repair/maintenance of machinery, equipment, weapons/sensors, spares supportability and trained HR. MTSS is also actively engaged in indigenous development and platform designing with the help of its partner.

Support to Surface Fleet

MTSS is providing support to CSTC (China Shipbuilding Trading Company Ltd) as its local representative since 2012 to coordinate and pursue supply/ support & technical assistance (RFQ, submission of quotes, monitoring of orders & deliveries, joint inspections & acceptance of stores) and swift release of payments through relevant authorities.

MTSS is also providing administrative & logistics support, coordination & liaison with MoDP, KSEW and PN as local representative of M/s HAVELSAN & M/s METEKSAN (Türkiye) for MILGEM project at Karachi Shipyard Engineering Works in addition to provision of trained manpower as per requirement.

Support to Aviation Fleet

MTSS as OEM authorized representative/ partner in Pakistan has provided repair and maintenance support to PN and PMSA aircraft/ equipment. Some of the activities undertaken are, overhaul of Rolls-Royce engines through M/s Standard Aero of Singapore and H&S Aviation of UK, spares supportability through M/s Britten Norman UK, procurement of Ground Support Equipment (GSE) through M/s Saywell UK and M/s MNA Technologies UK.

Support to Submarines Fleet

MTSS as local representative has been supporting Naval Group (Ex-DCNS) France during 2010-2022 for spares supportability of PN submarines & MCMVs, technical assistance, order management & tracking system and admin support. MTSS is capable to facilitate full spectrum support with respect to any upcoming submarine acquisition project.
