HIGH SPEED TARGET DRONE LAUNCHER

The UAV pneumatic launcher (designed and fabricated in-house) is a robust system engineered to launch Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) weighing between 20kg and 100kg. Utilizing pneumatic pressure, the launcher can propel UAVs at speeds ranging from 20 to 50m/s by adjusting the pressure in its vessel, which is tested to withstand pressures up to 60 bars.

Key features of the launcher include a 2-meter launch actuator and a total system length of 12.5 meters. It is also equipped with a drum-based friction braking system to ensure controlled deceleration after launch. The launcher has been thoroughly field- tested and proven reliable for a range of UAV deployment scenarios.

Technical Specifications:

  • MTOW of UAV : 20-120 kg
  • Mass of UAV Carriage : 10-25 kg
  • UAVs Launch velocity : 20-50 m/s
  • Launch angle : 5-15 degrees
  • Rail length : 12 m
  • Pulley ratio : 4
  • Max pneumatic force : 250 kN
  • Operating pressure : 10-80 bar
  • Actuator Stroke length : 1.875 m
  • Control system : PLC based
  • Controller : Touch HMI
