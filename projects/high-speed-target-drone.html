HIGH SPEED TARGET DRONE

Indigenously developed High-Speed Target Drone (HSTD) is aimed to serve as a high-speed target to train personnel for Live Weapon Firing (LWF) by ships Surface to Air Missiles, shore based SAMs and Anti-aircraft guns. It is deployed through pneumatic launcher and has parachute landing feature. System specifications are tabulated below:

Technical Specifications:

  • Cruise speed : 100 m/s
  • Max speed : 120 m/s
  • Stall speed : 25 m/s
  • Launch speed : 28 m/s
  • Cross wind : 10 m/s
  • Endurance : 20 min
  • Payload Capacity : 1.2 kg
  • MTOW : 20 kg
  • Thrust : 220 N
  • Engine type : Turbojet engine
  • Fuel type : Jet-A 1
  • Overall Length : 2 m
  • Overall Width : 1.4 m
  • Launch method : Pneumatic launcher
  • Recovery : Parachute recovery
  • Data link range : 30 km
